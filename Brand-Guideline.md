# Brand-Guideline.md — Constitutional Intelligence Platform

# Brand Identity

## Brand Personality
The platform should feel:
- Intelligent
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Calm
- Authoritative
- Accessible
- Premium

Avoid looking:
- Corporate generic
- Overly colorful
- Government outdated
- Tech startup cliché

---

# Visual Direction

Blend:
- Legal sophistication
- Modern AI aesthetics
- Academic elegance
- Minimalist interfaces

Design inspiration:
- Premium editorial products
- Luxury productivity apps
- Modern legal platforms

---

# UI Component Style

## Buttons

### Primary Button
- Sapphire background
- White text
- 14px radius
- Subtle hover elevation

### Secondary Button
- Transparent
- Thin border
- Soft hover glow

---

# Card Design

Cards should use:
- Soft shadows
- Large radius
- Thin borders
- Glass-like depth

### Radius
20px

### Shadow
Very soft, premium shadows only.

---

# Motion Guidelines

## Animation Style
- Smooth
- Slow-medium speed
- Elegant
- Non-aggressive

Avoid:
- Bouncy animations
- Overly playful effects

---

# Spacing System

## Base Unit
8px

Recommended:
- 8
- 16
- 24
- 32
- 48
- 64

---

# Iconography

Use:
- Lucide Icons
OR
- Phosphor Icons

Style:
- Thin strokes
- Rounded corners
- Minimalist

---

# Voice UI Branding

Voice player should feel:
- Calm
- Sophisticated
- Clean

Use:
- Pulsing waveform animations
- Teal highlights
- Soft glow effects

---

# Reading Experience Guidelines

## Legal Text Formatting
- Wide line spacing
- Comfortable margins
- Sticky progress indicators

## Reading Width
Maximum:
760px

---

# Dark Mode Design

Dark mode is critical.

Use:
- Deep navy backgrounds
- Soft contrast
- Avoid pure black

---

# Accessibility Design

Support:
- High contrast mode
- Reduced motion mode
- Dyslexia-friendly spacing
- Keyboard navigation

---

# Mobile Design Standards

Prioritize:
- Thumb-friendly navigation
- Sticky bottom controls
- Audio-first interactions

---

# Illustration Style

Use:
- Abstract legal imagery
- Constitutional symbolism
- Elegant gradients
- Minimal line art

Avoid:
- Cartoon visuals
- Generic stock photos

---

# Logo Direction

Possible concepts:
- Scales of justice

Style:
- Minimal
- Premium

---

# Sound Design

UI sounds should be:
- Soft
- Minimal
- Professional

Avoid:
- Loud clicks
- Gaming-style sounds

The platform should feel like:
- A premium legal intelligence system

- Trust
